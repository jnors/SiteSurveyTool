# iOS PWA Camera FAQ

## Camera Access
- Ensure the app is added to the home screen for best stability.
- Allow camera permissions on first use.

## Common Issues
- Large original photos can cause memory pressure. SST resizes to 1080p JPEG to keep capture and previews reliable.
- If the capture sheet does not appear, switch camera to “Back” and try again.

## Tips
- Take multiple close-up photos for detail rather than relying on one ultra-high-res shot.
- If a photo appears rotated, retake within the PWA; SST applies orientation during processing.
